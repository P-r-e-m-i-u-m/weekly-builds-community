# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this community harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, or identity.

## Standards

**Do:**
- Be kind and respectful
- Give honest, constructive feedback
- Celebrate others' wins genuinely
- Help newcomers feel welcome

**Don't:**
- Spam or post low-effort entries just for recognition
- Harass or demean other contributors
- Claim credit for work that isn't yours

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to [@P-r-e-m-i-u-m](https://github.com/P-r-e-m-i-u-m). PRs or comments that violate this code will be removed. Repeated violations result in being blocked.

Adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
